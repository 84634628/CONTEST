The authors encourage the toolkit users for their contributions. We believe that the supplied platform can provide an easy way of custom transceiver design modelling and can be easily extended in the future.

If you use this toolkit in your own research, please cite the following paper:
Y. Audzevich, P. M. Watts, A. West, A. Mujumdar, S. W. Moore, and A. W. Moore, "Power Optimized Transceivers for Future Switched Networks," IEEE Transactions on VLSI, in press.

If you are planning to contribute to the project, please contact us for more information and assistance.
